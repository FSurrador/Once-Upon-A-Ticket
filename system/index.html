"Once Upon A Ticket" is a Cinema Ticket Generator which turns you into a character of your favorite movie. After you generate your Ticket, you can choose the format you want and download it. Share it to your followers on Instagram. There's also the option to print it and start a big collection. This app still doesn't work 100% of the times, so it might take some tries on your favorite movie.
